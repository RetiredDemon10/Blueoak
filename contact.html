Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What is the minimum investment amount?

Our minimum investment amount starts at $1,000 for our Starter Plan. We offer various investment plans to accommodate different budget levels and investment goals.

How do I get started with BlueOak Capitals?

Getting started is easy! Simply fill out our contact form, schedule a free consultation, or call us directly. Our team will assess your financial goals and recommend the best investment strategy for you.

Are my investments insured and protected?

Yes, we are SIPC protected up to $500,000 per account. We are also fully licensed and regulated by the U.S. Department of Treasury and SEC, ensuring the highest level of security for your investments.

What fees do you charge?

Our fee structure is transparent and competitive. Management fees vary by plan and investment amount, typically ranging from 0.5% to 1.5% annually. There are no hidden fees, and we provide detailed fee disclosure before you invest.

Can I withdraw my money anytime?

Yes, you can request withdrawals at any time. However, some investment plans have specific terms and conditions. We recommend discussing your liquidity needs during the consultation to choose the right plan for you.
